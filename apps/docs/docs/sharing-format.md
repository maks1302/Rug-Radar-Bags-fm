---

Use this style for X/Discord/Telegram.

## Template

`$TOKEN — Risk X/100 (Label)`
`Top flags: <flag1>, <flag2>`
`Liquidity: $X | Top10: Y% | DYOR`

## Example

`$ABC — Risk 63/100 (High)`
`Top flags: Mint authority active, Top10 concentration high`
`Liquidity: $58k | Top10: 69% | DYOR`

Keep it factual and avoid certainty language.
