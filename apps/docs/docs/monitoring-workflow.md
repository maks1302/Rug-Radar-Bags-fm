## Why Use Monitoring

Single scans are snapshots. Monitoring catches shifts.

## Workflow

1. Create watch:
`Watch <token> with your thresholds.`

2. Check deltas regularly:
`What changed since last check for <token>?`

3. Focus on trigger events:
- Risk score increase
- Liquidity drop
- Holder concentration increase
- Authority change
- Honeypot flip

4. Act on trend, not one candle.

## Suggested Cadence

- High volatility: every 15-30 minutes
- Normal conditions: every few hours
