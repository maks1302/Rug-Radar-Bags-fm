## Risk Score

- 0-25: Low Risk
- 26-50: Medium Risk
- 51-75: High Risk
- 76-100: Extreme Risk

Higher score means more risk, not guaranteed outcome.

## Confidence

Confidence reflects data completeness for that run.

- Higher confidence = more complete source coverage
- Lower confidence = more uncertainty

## Red Flags vs Bullish Signals

- Red flags highlight potential failure modes
- Bullish signals show quality indicators

Treat both as context, not certainty.

## Source Notes

If a source is unavailable, reports still return partial output.
Always consider missing data before making decisions.
